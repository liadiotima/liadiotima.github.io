About Me

Hi, I’m Lia, and I’ve been creating content since I was 14. What started as pure intuition and a lot of ADHD-driven creativity quickly grew into a community of over 250,000 followers.

Over the past 1.5 years, I’ve turned that passion into professional UGC, combining nearly a decade of digital experience with my background in Psychology.

I don’t just create content. I create connection. By understanding how people think, feel, and scroll, I produce scroll-stopping UGC that feels authentic, relatable, and impactful. Every piece of content is designed to capture attention, spark emotion, and build genuine trust with your audience.

What I love most is the creative process. I enjoy turning a brand’s vision into engaging content that feels human, natural, and true to its values while still driving real results.
